Slowly you unshoulder your gun, slip the safety catch, and lower the barrel towards the ground. The rattlesnake darts its head forward and attempts to bury its fangs in your ankle. Its attack is all over, though, the moment you squeeze the trigger: at such close range your gunfire rips the snake apart (remember to erase the appropriate amount of ammunition from your Ammo Pouch).

You must now take a Drink (or lose 3 ENDURANCE points) before you climb back into your roadster and return to the convoy.
