You pull the bolt of your G-12, priming it to fire, and then rest its stubby barrel on the rollbar of your roadster to steady your aim. At 200 yards your target appears little more than 1/16 inch high. It will demand great skill and considerable luck to hit such a small target with a machine pistol.

Pick a number from the Random Number Table and add to it your current Shooting skill level. Now decide how many bursts of fire you intend to launch at the target (minimum: one burst; maximum: five bursts). For every burst, add one point to your current score.

If your total score is now 11 or less, turn to 164.

If it is 12 or more, turn to 58.
