20

The instant you tap the number 20 on the keypad,2 there is a sharp click and the steel door swings inwards to reveal the shelved walls of a strongroom beyond. You smile with satisfaction at having cracked the university’s door code: the number in the last column of each row is the sum of the numbers in the first two columns, minus the number in the third column.

Curious as to what lies within, you enter the strongroom and inspect its dusty shelves. This concrete vault contains the documentation of experiments and small quantities of what were once considered precious metals—gold, platinum, silver, and titanium. After a thorough search you find three items that you may be able to put to practical use:

  • Geiger Counter
  • Altimeter
  • Radiation Suit (occupies two item spaces in the Backpack)

If you wish to keep any of these items, amend your Action Chart accordingly.

To leave the strongroom and return to the convoy, turn to 29.

[2] This is the correct answer to the keypad puzzle in Section 152.
