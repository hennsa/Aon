Quickly you retrieve your weapon and step outside into the corridor. There, lying next to the siren, you see the guard’s machine pistol. (If you decide to take this weapon, you discover that its magazine contains twenty-four rounds of 9mm ammunition.)

You pick up the siren and switch it off as you run towards the iron staircase. You are halfway up the steps when you see the door to the observation block start to open.
