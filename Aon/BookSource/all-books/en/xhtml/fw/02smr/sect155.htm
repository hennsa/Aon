With dismay, Kate tells you that she can find no anti-toxins in your Medi-kit. Using blankets dragged from the store room, she tries to make you as comfortable as possible as the first shivers of fever begin to grip your pain-wracked body. She cleans the inflamed wound and tries to comfort you as best she can while, over the next three hours, your body battles to overcome the toxin in your blood: lose 8 ENDURANCE points.

If you are still alive after sustaining such a grievous shock to your system, turn to 291.
