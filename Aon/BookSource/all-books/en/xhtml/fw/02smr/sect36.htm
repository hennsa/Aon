For a moment the clansman shudders at your feet as his last breath escapes from between his blood-flecked lips. Then his eyes glaze over and he succumbs to the endless sleep of death. Kate moves forward to check that he is no longer a threat and to search his body. She keeps what she needs: his machine pistol, a quantity of ammunition, and his water canteen, and offers the rest of his possessions to you:

  • Enough food for one Meal
  • Enough sulfonamides for one Medi-kit unit
  • Flick Knife (2)
  • Compass
  • Binoculars

If you keep any of these items, remember to adjust your Action Chart accordingly.

As soon as you are ready, you retrace your way along the defile and return to the surface.
