Footnotes

[1] (Equipment) If you have already completed any of the previous Freeway Warrior adventures, it is unclear whether you may replenish your supplies before starting this adventure or whether you can only carry over the supplies you carried at the end of the previous adventure. It seems reasonable that you would be able to restock your supplies while in El Paso.

[2] (Weapons) It is reasonably clear that the Machine Pistol should not be used if you have fewer than 6 rounds of 9mm ammunition.

For some readers, however, it may seem counterintuitive to have ammunition left but be unable to use the weapon. We suggest to these readers that when 9mm ammunition falls below 6 rounds, the Machine Pistol can be treated as a Pistol (cf. Section 333 of Highway Holocaust). While this is not a perfect solution, it is a reasonable compromise.
