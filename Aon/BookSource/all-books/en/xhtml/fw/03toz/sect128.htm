Marine Knott takes the wheel with the captain riding beside him in the cab. Meanwhile, you and the sergeant climb in the back and take up firing positions at the tailgate. Steadily Knott drives towards Columbus, keeping the speed down to avoid arousing too much suspicion. You are sixty yards from the barricade when the captain shouts through the cab partition: ‘Hold on men—we’re going in!’

You exchange an anxious glance with Sergeant Haskell as the engine roars and the truck picks up speed.
