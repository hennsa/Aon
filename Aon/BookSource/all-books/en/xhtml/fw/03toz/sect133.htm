Swiftly you raise your pistol, cupped in both hands, ignoring the bullets that are whistling past as you take aim at the clan commander. For a brief instant, his eyes widen with shock when he sees the muzzle of your Beretta pointing directly at his chest. He starts to pull away, but before he can take cover, you squeeze off your first shot.

Add together your Shooting skill total and your current ENDURANCE points score.

If your total is 8 or less, turn to 99.

If it is 9 or more, turn to 296.
