Once you wipe away the thick layer of dust that blankets every piece of the merchandise in this store, you realize that it is stocked with several items that may prove useful during your ride to Tucson.

You collect together an armful of goods and lay them out by the door before choosing which ones to keep. You have discovered the following items:

  • Rifle
  • Shotgun
  • Six rounds of 7.62mm ammunition
  • Twelve rounds of 12-gauge ammunition
  • Hunting Knife (2)
  • Bowie Knife (3)
  • Solar Torch
  • Fishing Tackle
  • Tool Kit
  • Rope
  • Blanket

If you wish to keep any of these items, remember to make the appropriate adjustments to your Action Chart.
