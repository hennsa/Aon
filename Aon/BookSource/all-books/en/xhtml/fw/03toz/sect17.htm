You roll away from the dead clansman and scramble to your feet in time to see his partner escaping towards the exit ramp. You must prevent him from raising the alarm, so you sprint after him as fast as your aching legs will allow. You catch up with him near the top of the ramp and dive forward, dragging him to his knees with a flying tackle. You ready yourself for a fight but such action proves unnecessary: with a loud crack, his head hits the concrete as he falls, and he is knocked unconscious.
