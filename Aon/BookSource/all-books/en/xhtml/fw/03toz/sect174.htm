Knott acts as lookout while you take a look inside the cab. You find nothing of use, even the ignition key is missing, so you abandon the search and hurry to the rear of the truck. The doors are unlocked and inside you discover several open-topped boxes containing the following items:

  • Enough food for five Meals
  • Blanket
  • Solar Torch
  • File
  • Tool Kit
  • Air Pump
  • Enough water for three Drinks
  • Bandages and surgical tape, sufficient enough for two Medi-kit units
  • Rope

If you decide to keep any of these items, remember to adjust your Action Chart accordingly.

Having satisfied your curiosity, you leave the truck and motion to Marine Knott to continue.
