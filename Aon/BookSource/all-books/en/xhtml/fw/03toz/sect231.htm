You brake heavily and Mad Dog’s speedster crosses in front of you to hit the barrier with a terrific screech. Metal twists and sparks fly from the fender as he scrapes along the crash barrier at nearly 100 miles an hour. You pull back and overtake him on the right, powering your roadster away as he fights to free himself from the barrier’s magnet-like grip.
