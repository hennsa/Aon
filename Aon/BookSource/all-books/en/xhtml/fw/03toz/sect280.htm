Suddenly, a concealed door in the wall behind you bursts open and in rushes the bearded man with a pistol clenched firmly in his hand.

‘Drop your guns!’ he commands. ‘Drop them now or the only talkin’ you’ll be doin’ is to the Devil!’

Slowly you let your gun fall to the floor, and then you raise your hands and turn to face your ambusher.
