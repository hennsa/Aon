You tell the others that you think the team should attempt to break through the Columbus road block.

‘Well, it looks like we got ourselves what the French call an “impasse”,’ says the captain, as he reaches to the breast pocket of his combat jacket. ‘There’s only one way to solve this … ’ So saying, he withdraws a coin—a commemorative AD2000 silver dollar.

‘Heads we hit Columbus; tails we avoid it,’ he says, and flips the coin into the air.
