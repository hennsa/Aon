77

You have inadvertently stumbled upon a remote survivalist colony, which undoubtedly had the foresight to prepare a shelter long before the holocaust occurred. Your arrival and means of entry must have convinced them that they are under concerted attack, and they have sought sanctuary in the lower levels of their shelter.

You share your thoughts with Knott and he agrees with your assessment of the situation. Rather than risk it ending in tragedy, he calls out to whoever is hiding below, assuring them that you wish them no harm and asking them to come out and talk with you.
