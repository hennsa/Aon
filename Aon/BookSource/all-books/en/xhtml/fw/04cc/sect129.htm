Nobody sees you leave Gila Bend. Hidden by the storm, you drive south, along Highway 85 towards the obscured peaks of the Sauceda Mountains. Occasionally the storm lets up long enough for you to make out a road sign, or the ruins of a motel, but for the most part of an hour and a half you see nothing but swirling dust on the road ahead.

During your lonely drive you must take a Drink (or lose 3 ENDURANCE points) and eat a Meal (or lose 3 ENDURANCE points).
