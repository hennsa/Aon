Although your gunfire misses the clansman, it penetrates the side of the pick-up truck and tears a hole in a metal container full of industrial blasting powder, which is standing in the back. (Remember to deduct the relevant number of rounds from your Ammo Pouch.) A spark, created when the container is holed, ignites the powder with devastating effect. There is a jarring boom, and then the truck erupts into a blazing fireball of orange and scarlet flame.
