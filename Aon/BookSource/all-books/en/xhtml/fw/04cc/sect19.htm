19

Following your hastily scribbled directions, you drive around the empty streets of Ajo for nearly an hour before you find the town’s Memorial Hospital. Once inside, you search the reception area and uncover a plan that reveals the location of the medicine storage facility. It lies at the end of a maze of corridors which are signposted but unlit.

If you possess a Solar Torch, a Box of Matches, or a Cigarette Lighter, turn to 190.

If you do not possess any of these items, turn to 44.
