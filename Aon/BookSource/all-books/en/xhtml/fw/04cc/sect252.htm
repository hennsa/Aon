The colony refuses to trust Cookie, and they will not allow you to risk his betraying the convoy’s presence in the area. They tell you to retie his hands and deal with the clan patrol as best you can. Rickenbacker tries to argue but he cannot make them change their minds. Before they sign off, they tell you to maintain radio silence until the bridge is clear, just in case the clansmen are monitoring the frequencies.
