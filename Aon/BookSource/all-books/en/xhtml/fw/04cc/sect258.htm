The speed of your reactions saves you from the snake’s vicious bite. Its venomous jaw snaps shut on thin air and, before you have a chance to retaliate, it drops down off the shelf and slithers away into the shadows, its tail rattling furiously.

Still trembling with the shock of your close encounter, you take your hard-won case of food and return with it to the roadster.
