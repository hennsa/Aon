You dive behind the truck to avoid getting cut down by the hail of bullets that are spewing from the barrel of the clansman’s machine pistol. Unfortunately, several of the slugs penetrate the side of the pick-up truck and tear into a metal container full of industrial blasting powder that is standing in the back. A spark, caused by one of the slugs, ignites the powder with devastating effect. There is a brilliant flash and you are consumed by a searing blast of heat as the truck and its contents are blown to pieces.

Tragically, your journey and your life end here at the Gila River.
