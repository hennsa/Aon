You notice that a side door to the restaurant cabin is open, and cautiously you move across the barbecue area towards it. You have taken less than a dozen steps when the clansman rises up from behind a brick range to your left. He has an ugly sneer fixed on his face and a machine pistol, poised to fire, cradled in his calloused hands.

If you wish to dive to the ground to avoid being shot, turn to 245.

If you wish to raise your gun to try to shoot him first, turn to 158.
