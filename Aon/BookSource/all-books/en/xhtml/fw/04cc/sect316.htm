As your group of defenders moves to intercept the raiding party, bursts of automatic fire kick up flurries of dust all around you. Their comrades are laying down a heavy covering fire, but it does not deter you from heading-off the raiding party’s attempt to circle behind your position.

When finally you reach the gravel road, you find yourself confronting the raiding party head-on and out in the open, where there is little or no cover. Instantly, your group finds itself in the middle of a desperate close-range firefight.

Pick a number from the Random Number Table and add to it your Shooting and Stealth skill scores.

If your total score is 9 or less, turn to 113.

If your total score is 10–14, turn to 294.

If your total score is 15 or more, turn to 255.
