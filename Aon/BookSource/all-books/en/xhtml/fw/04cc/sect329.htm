In a consummate display of driving skill, you brake heavily and spin the wheel hard over to the right to bring the roadster around in a screeching 180-degree turn. Then, the instant you are pointing in the opposite direction, you press the gas pedal to the floor and accelerate away. Your quick thinking and skilled driving have saved you from crashing into a wall of rock and from the rain of boulders that are now smashing down onto the highway.

As you put your foot down and speed away from the rockfall without injury, you thank Cutter Jacks for having had the patience and foresight to teach you this difficult manoeuvre.

Turn to 112.
