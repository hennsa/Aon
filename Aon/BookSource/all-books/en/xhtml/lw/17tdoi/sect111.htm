You will yourself to rise once more and slowly you return to the edge of the shaft, but your strength is waning and it is now impossible for you to simultaneously fight and maintain this spell.

‘For Sommerlund and the Kai!’ you shout, and leap among the monstrous Chaos-creatures crowding the stairs, lashing out in a wide arc as your feet touch down on the slimy steps. Gleefully, the unnatural horde close in around you for the kill.
