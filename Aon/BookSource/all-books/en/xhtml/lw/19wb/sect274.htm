You return to the stairs and descend to the foyer. Mindful of your enemy’s skills of deception and illusion, you take a closer look at the debris which blocks the stairs leading down. But you cannot detect any tracks or evidence that Wolf’s Bane has come this way. You are about to abandon the stairwell and leave the building when something catches your eye. Protruding from the dust that covers the top step is a small disc of iron engraved with runic symbols. (If you wish to keep this Iron Disc, record it on your Action Chart as a Special Item.)

To leave the building and continue, turn to 71.
