You take a few faltering steps and then fall headlong to the ground. The canopy of branches swirls above your head and then grows dimmer. You fight the sensation but you feel yourself rapidly slipping away into a deep sleep. Sadly, it is a sleep from which you will never awaken, for the blowpipe dart was tainted with a deadly poison.

Your life and your quest end here in the jungle of Shadaki.
