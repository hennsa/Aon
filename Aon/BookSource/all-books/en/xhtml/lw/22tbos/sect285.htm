285

You are within a dozen yards of a wall of jungle trees when suddenly the ground gives way beneath your feet and you fall headlong into a deep pit. Fortunately, the base of this pit trap does not contain sharpened stakes or any other such deadly contrivances, yet you do sustain several cuts and bruises when you land heavily upon its rocky base: lose 3 ENDURANCE points.

If you possess Kai-alchemy, turn to 336.

If you do not, turn to 55.
