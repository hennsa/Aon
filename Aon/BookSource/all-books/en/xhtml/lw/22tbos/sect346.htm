You draw upon your Animal Mastery in an attempt to calm these frightened and hungry creatures. They are susceptible to your powerful psychic commands, yet they produce an unexpected and adverse reaction. The sudden conflict of their instincts and feelings makes them howl with frustration. In a state of blind panic, the two wolf-like animals rush past you to disappear into the rain-swept jungle. Confident that they will not return, you settle yourself down upon a bed of soft mosses and drift off to sleep.
