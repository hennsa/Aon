Worried that the hunting dog may detect the uniforms and equipment that you have hidden in the closet, you draw upon your Magnakai Discipline of Animal Control and use it to block this canine’s sense of smell. Cursing the dog’s stubbornness, the handler drops the leash in disgust and then he and his companion set about searching the parlour and its adjoining rooms.
