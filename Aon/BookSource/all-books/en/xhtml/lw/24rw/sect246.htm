You glance at Delissa in the hope that she will say something to help you answer the sergeant’s question, but your Sixth Sense tells you that she is unable to do this. She does not know the sergeant’s cousin, nor the place where he has his shop.

If you wish to answer the sergeant’s question with a confident ‘Yes’, turn to 178.

If you wish to answer the sergeant’s question with a polite ‘No’, turn to 205.

If you wish to answer by saying that you have neither heard of his cousin nor the place where his shop is located, turn to 142.
