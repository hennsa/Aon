301

This cobblestoned street leads to a square watchtower at the place where Duadon’s south and east city walls meet. As you approach the tower, you notice a wheelwright’s workshop located on the corner. A grubby-faced youth stands in the doorway and he bids you good day as you approach.

‘Your regiment be in need of our services, sire?’ he asks keenly. You shake your head and go to walk on, but the young man tugs at your sleeve. He begs you to enter his father’s workshop and examine for yourself the quality of his craftsmanship.

If you wish to enter the wheelwright’s workshop, turn to 222.

If you choose not to enter, you may continue along Kettle Street by turning to 81.
