You leave the glassblower’s workshop and turn the corner into North Watch Lane. Here you discover a dozen small shops that trade in weapons and armour. Most of the items they offer for sale are poorly crafted, but there is one shop which specializes in fine weapons imported from all over Magnamund. Your eye is caught by an impressive collection of Siyenese bows and Lourdenian swords on display in its front window.

If you wish to enter this armoury, turn to 237.

If you choose to continue along North Watch Lane, turn to 147.
