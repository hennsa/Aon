35

The unarmed Vorka defend the bell-chamber with great tenacity. Before you are able to cut through their ranks, the first of the armoured Vorka regiment fall upon you from the side. You fight like a demon, slaying five of the enemy in as many blows, but in so doing you are drawn further away from the bell-chamber. The rune is now beyond arm’s length and more of the armoured Vorka are moving to cut you off.

If you possess Kai-alchemy and wish to use it, turn to 69.

If you possess Elementalism and have attained the rank of Kai Grand Defender, turn to 316.

If you possess a Bow and wish to use it, turn to 238.

If you possess neither of these skills, nor a Bow, or if you have yet to attain the required level of Kai Mastery, or should you choose not to use any of them, turn instead to 73.
