The Storms of Chai

Joe Dever

234

Your Arrow enters the creature’s gaping mouth and penetrates the base of its brain, killing it instantly. The second beast halts dead in its tracks and looks down at the twitching body of its fallen companion with a mix of fear and anger. Hurriedly, you draw another Arrow to your Bow and take aim at the stationary creature.

Pick a number from the Random Number Table. If you have Grand Weaponmastery with Bow, add 2 (instead of 5) to the number you have chosen. If you have attained the rank of Kai Grand Defender or higher, add another 2.

If your total is now 5 or lower, turn to 131.

If your total is 6 or higher, turn to 243.
