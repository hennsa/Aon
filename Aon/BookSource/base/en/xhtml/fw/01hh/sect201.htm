You recognize at once the chemical symbol for sodium chloride—common salt: you have stumbled upon a huge stockpile of a substance that is vital to human survival, yet one that is becoming harder to find with every passing day. In your excitement you give a shout and can barely stop yourself from dancing around the crate in sheer delight.

If you wish to take as many tubs of salt as you can carry and then return to the convoy, turn to 29.

If you wish to investigate the steel door before leaving the basement, turn to 152.
