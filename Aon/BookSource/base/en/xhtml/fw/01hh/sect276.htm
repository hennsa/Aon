You pull the pin and hurl your grenade through the open window. It bounces noisily on the tiled floor of the diner, startling the men and causing them to cry out in surprise. Moments later their cries are lost amid the deafening roar of an explosion. Silence follows but you wait for the acrid smoke to clear before venturing inside to see what remains of your would-be killers.
