You crouch beneath the window and listen to the voices of the two men inside the diner. They think that you are still hiding behind your car, and you hear them speculating as to whether or not you are wounded. You have the element of surprise and you decide to use it to your advantage by launching a sudden attack.

If you wish to enter the diner by the rear door, turn to 51.

If you choose to enter the diner by the front door, turn to 203.
