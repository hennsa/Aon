320

You pump a cartridge into the breach and take aim at the leading dog. You fire as it leaps, the full force of your shot catching it squarely in the chest. Its lifeless body somersaults backwards into the rest of the pack.

Remember to deduct a round of 12-gauge ammunition from your Ammo Pouch.

If you wish to fire a second round at the rabid dogs and are able to do so, turn to 190.

If you have run out of ammunition, or if you choose not to fire a second round, turn to 64.
