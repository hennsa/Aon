You hurl the grenade and it lands neatly in the centre of the ammunition chest. Without waiting for it to explode, you turn and sprint for the safety of the yucca forest. As you are running from the barricade, a clansman catches sight of you and opens fire with his machine pistol. Bullets kick up the dirt around your feet, but you manage to reach cover without sustaining any wounds.
