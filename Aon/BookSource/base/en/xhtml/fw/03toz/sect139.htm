You reach the trapdoor and stare down the flight of wooden steps into the darkness below. Although you cannot see or hear anything, you sense that someone is there.

If you wish to assure whoever is hiding below that you mean them no harm and that all you wish to do is talk with them, turn to 280.

If you prefer to demand that they throw down their weapons and come out with their hands up, turn to 68.
