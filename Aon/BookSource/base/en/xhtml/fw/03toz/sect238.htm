You investigate the ground surrounding the spring and discover that it is lush with green vegetation, mosses, and damp earth imprinted with many animal tracks. There are no signs of carcasses or dead foliage, which would indicate that the spring is contaminated.

Now that you are sure that it is safe to do so you drink from the spring, refill your water canteen, and wash in the cool, clean water: restore 4 ENDURANCE points.
