You dismount, wheel the motorcycle to the edge of the gully, and then let it tumble down the side. You watch it roll to the bottom before approaching the rockfall and beginning to climb. The mound stands more than sixty feet high, and is as steep and as sheer as a granite mountain in places. You have been climbing less than a few minutes when you have difficulty maintaining your grip. Pick a number from the Random Number Table and add to it your Field Craft skill total. If you possess a Rope or a Tow Chain, add 2 points to your total.

If your total score is now 9 or less, turn to 123.

If it is 10 or more, turn to 216.
