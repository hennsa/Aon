Fixedly you concentrate on the area of the forest that you have picked as your target destination. The more you think about what lies ahead, the more you shiver with dread at having to risk your life running such a dangerous gauntlet of fire.

The chance that the sniper will fail to hit you is very slim indeed. To increase the odds in your favour, you will need to distract his aim.
