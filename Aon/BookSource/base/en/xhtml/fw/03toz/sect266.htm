A rush of adrenalin fires your reflexes and you throw yourself to the floor as the muzzle of the gun belches flame. You avoid a fatal wound but you do not escape entirely without injury. As you fall, one of the bullets cuts a stinging groove across the side of your skull: lose 4 ENDURANCE points.

Angered by the wound you have received, and how close you came to death, you struggle to your feet just in time to see the spy-hole slam shut.

If you wish to attempt to barge open the door, turn to 186.

If you wish to shoot open the lock and are able to do so, turn to 341.
