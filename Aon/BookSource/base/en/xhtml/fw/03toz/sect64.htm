64

Mounds of dust lie heaped before the closed doors of this derelict town, stirred to lazy movement by the warm breeze that sweeps down from the surrounding mountains. You park your motorcycle before a burnt-out gas station, and then walk slowly along the main street, peering into the grimy windows as you pass. Only three buildings strike you as worthy of further investigation: a hardware store, a drug store, and a radio repair shop.

If you wish to investigate the hardware store, turn to 152.

If you choose to enter the drug store, turn to 272.

If you decide to take a closer look at the radio shop, turn to 229.

If you prefer to abandon your search and return to your motorcycle and leave Gleeson, turn to 50.
