The commander dives aside fast enough to avoid being hit, but one of his men is not so lucky. As he runs around the corner, he catches your gunfire full in the chest and is thrown backwards to the ground. (Remember to deduct the relevant number of rounds from your Ammo Pouch.)

Angered by your attempt to kill him, the hard-faced clan commander unslings his rifle and throws himself down behind the man you have just shot, using his body as a shield.

If you wish to fire at the commander before he can fire at you, turn to 78.

If you decide to take cover behind the tailgate, turn to 301.
