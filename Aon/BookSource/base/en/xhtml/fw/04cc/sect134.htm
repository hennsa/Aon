Bullets hit the ground in a line that follows that of your dive. In your desperation to avoid being hit, you misjudge your dive and smash your chin against the edge of the hollow. As you rebound from the collision, three of the clansman’s bullets hit you in the chest and arm: lose 10 ENDURANCE points.

If you have survived this grievous wounding, turn to 229.
