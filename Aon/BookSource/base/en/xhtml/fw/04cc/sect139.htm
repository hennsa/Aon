Acting instinctively, you throw yourself out of the path of the falling machine and narrowly avoid being crushed to death when it slams into the floor and disintegrates.

You sustain cuts to your hands and knees when you land among the debris (lose 2 ENDURANCE points), but at least you emerge from the warehouse with your life—and the case of food—intact.

Still trembling with the shock of your close encounter, you take up your hard-won food and carry it back to your roadster.
