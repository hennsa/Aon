An ounce of lead shot hits the clansman in the chest, killing him instantly. (Remember to deduct a round of 12-gauge ammunition from your Ammo Pouch.) The sight of his gross body tumbling backwards down the slope sends a shock wave through his confederates, and at once their attack falters. Encouraged by their sudden change of heart, you watch as they retreat towards their trucks, which are parked in the centre of the freeway.
