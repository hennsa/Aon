Driven by fear and desperation, you attack the padlock and chains that are preventing your escape. Acrid fumes fill the room with a sickening stench that makes you dizzy and nauseous: lose 5 ENDURANCE points.

Finally your efforts are rewarded: the padlock breaks and the fire exit door bursts open. Amid billowing clouds of grey smoke, you stagger out onto a fire escape landing and unsteadily descend a rusty iron staircase to a parking lot below. As clean air fills your lungs your strength returns, enabling you to flee the area before a massive explosion destroys the warehouse completely.
