You throw yourself forwards and roll clear as the clansmen’s car glances your roadster’s rear fender. You suffer gashes to your hands and knees (lose 1 ENDURANCE point), but your swift action saves you from being crushed to death. As you recover you see the clan car smash through the freeway crash barrier. With a sickening crunch, it hits a telegraph pole, overturns, and then spills its occupants out onto the sun-baked earth as it rolls to the bottom of a steep embankment.
