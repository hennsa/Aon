You flip open your pouch, remove a handful of pain killers, and throw them out onto the ground beside the truck’s cab. Immediately the three Crazies leap on the scattered pills and fight each other like hungry rats to get more than a fair share. The sight of their animalistic behaviour turns your stomach, and when Rickenbacker prods you in the ribs and says, ‘C’mon, Cal. Let’s go,’ you gladly reach for the ignition and slam the gas pedal flat to the floor.

Erase three units from your Medi-kit.
