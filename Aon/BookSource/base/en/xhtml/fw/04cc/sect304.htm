Against your better judgement, you leave Cookie tied up in the cab of the pick-up truck before you begin the unenviable task of clearing the bridge. A five-mile detour takes you across the Gila River and enables you to approach the clansmen from the north, the direction in which they would be least expecting an attack. After observing their small encampment, you decide that the first priority must be to destroy their radio, which is located inside their truck.
