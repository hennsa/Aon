69

You kick the shop door and it creaks open unwillingly. Inside you find that the store is virtually empty, having already been looted by clansmen eager to get their hands on its stock of off-road motorbikes.

A brief search of a workshop at the back of the premises yields just two items which may be of use:

  • Tool kit
  • Engine Oil

If you decide to keep either or both of these items, remember to adjust your Action Chart accordingly.

To leave the shop and rejoin Rickenbacker, turn to 300.
