You walk along the banks of Lake Shenwu. The easiest point to cross is where the Azan River starts, for Lake Shenwu is its source. The waters here still glint a dirty yellow colour, although the luminous mist is less dense. You lose 1 ENDURANCE point as a sleepy feeling begins to overtake you.

If you wish to try to wade across the River Azan, turn to 17.

If you would rather try to jump across the stepping stones, turn to 242.
