You see a deep pool of greenish liquid called Leafwater, a strange and unique plant. When Phinomel Pods are cast into the pool the watery substance is transformed. For every pod you cast into it, add 1 to your COMBAT SKILL when you dip a weapon into it. You may affect your Wizard’s Staff in this way.22

Turn to 359.

[22] It appears that for every Phinomel Pod that you possess and wish to throw into the Leafwater pool, you may permanently raise your COMBAT SKILL by 1 when using your Wizard’s Staff. Remember to delete these Phinomel Pods from your Action Chart.
