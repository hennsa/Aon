A gigantic creeping reptile hangs from the ceiling, gripping the stone with its long, curved claws. Its huge oval eyes flicker hungrily as it picks up the scent of live human flesh—your flesh! It is poised to strike, and you cannot evade its attack.

Kwaraz: COMBAT SKILL 19   ENDURANCE 30

The giant reptile is very susceptible to psychic power, so if you have the Kai Discipline of Mindblast, add 4 instead of the normal 2 to your basic COMBAT SKILL for the duration of the fight.

If you win the combat, turn to 40.
