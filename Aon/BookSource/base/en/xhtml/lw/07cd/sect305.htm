The designs are woven from pure gold and silver, studded with gems and semi-precious stones. You marvel at the craftsmanship and beauty of these tapestries—they must be worth many thousands of crowns. One gem in particular catches your eye—a large Diamond. Unable to resist the temptation, you prise it from the cloth and drop it into your pocket (mark this as a Special Item on your Action Chart), before leaving the hall by the door.
