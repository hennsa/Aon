With the speed and grace of a hunting leopard you launch yourself at the Drakkarim rider. Pick a number from the Random Number Table. If you have the Magnakai Discipline of Huntmastery, add 3 to the number you have picked.

If your total is now 0–3, turn to 237.

If it is 4 or more, turn to 149.
