‘Quickly, Prarg!’ you shout, as the advancing pack spreads out, attempting to surround you. ‘Stand back-to-back. Whatever you do, don’t let them separate us or we’re done for!’

The captain obeys your command and, as the first of the snarling war-dogs comes leaping through the air, you steel yourselves to receive its manic attack.
