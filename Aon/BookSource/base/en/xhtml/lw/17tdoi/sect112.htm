112

The metal door is sheathed with ice and there is no handle visible—only a hexagonal-shaped keyhole. You utter the words of the Brotherhood Spell Lightning Hand and point your index finger at this keyhole. A tingling sensation runs along your arm, culminating in a burst of blue-white energy which arcs from your fingertip to the lock.

Use of this spell so soon after recovering consciousness costs you 2 ENDURANCE points.

Adjust your Action Chart accordingly before turning to 174.
