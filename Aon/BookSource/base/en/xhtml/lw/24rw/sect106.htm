Anxious that the hunting dog may detect your hiding place, your draw upon your Magnakai Discipline of Animal Control and use it to block the creature’s sense of smell. The dog becomes confused and he refuses to move from the parlour door, despite his handler’s rough attempts to drag him into the premises. Cursing the dog’s stubbornness, the handler drops his leash and the two Eldenorans set about searching the parlour unassisted.
