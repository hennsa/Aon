Quickly you switch your weapon to your left hand and point at the serpent’s head with your right. You speak the words of the Brotherhood Spell Net and a gout of sticky fluid jets from your outstretched fingers to engulf the giant snake as it launches its attack. The gluey fluid fills its mouth and flows around its neck to splash against the ceiling and wall. It solidifies instantly and holds the creature back, preventing it from sinking its deadly fangs into your chest.

If you now wish to attack the serpent, turn to 206.

If you decide to evade the serpent while it is held secure by your sticky net, turn to 329.
