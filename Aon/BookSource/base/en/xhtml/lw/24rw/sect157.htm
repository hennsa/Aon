Courageously you rail against the pain that lingers in your body and mind following the creature’s mental attack, and you raise your Kai Weapon defiantly. You sense that the assault upon you has temporarily depleted Zorkaan’s psychic powers, and the shadowy creature is now retreating towards the rune in order to replenish its energy.
