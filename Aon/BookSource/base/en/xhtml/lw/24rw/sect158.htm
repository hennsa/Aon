You walk past the scribe’s table and ascend the staircase to the floor above where you discover a corridor lined with marble statues. A black door with a shiny lacquered surface stands at the far end of the corridor, held secure by a combination lock. The three copper dials that protrude from its lock are arranged in the shape of a triangle. You place your ear to the door at the centre of this triangle and, by the use of your Magnakai skills, you are able to hear the delicate sound of the tumbler mechanisms as you turn each dial. By turning each of the dials to its correct setting you will cause the lock to disengage and the door will open.

You determine that the setting for the first dial is identical to the number given to the Kai name prefix True. The second dial setting is identical to the number given to the Kai name suffix Hawk. And the third dial setting is identical to the number of Kai Weapons that you are permitted to carry and use.

Study the above details carefully. When you think you know the solution to all three settings, write the answers in order and then turn to the section that is the same as your answer.7

If you should guess incorrectly, or if you cannot determine the correct solution, turn instead to 26.

[7] The section corresponding to the correct answer will have a footnote confirming that it is indeed correct.
