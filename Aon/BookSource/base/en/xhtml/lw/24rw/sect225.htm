You are able to identify the purplish mould that stains the ceiling of this tunnel. It is Ghilev, a fungus that can induce sleep in humans and animals when swallowed. If you wish to scrape some of this Ghilev from the ceiling, you may gather enough for one dose and keep it in your Backpack.
