You draw upon your Kai Sixth Sense in the hope it will reveal to you the whereabouts of the runes. You sense that they are present here in the inner keep, but you are unable to pinpoint their precise location. Undeterred by this, you resolve to explore the keep and track them down. As you scan the three exits from this entrance hall, you pray to the Goddess Ishir to help you choose a route which will lead swiftly to the accursed runes.

If you wish to enter the portal to your left, turn to 314.

If you choose to enter the portal to your right, turn to 7.

If you decide to climb the stairs to the floor above, turn to 158.
