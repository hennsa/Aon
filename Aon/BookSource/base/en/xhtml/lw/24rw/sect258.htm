You call upon your Magnakai skill of Invisibility to mask your body scent; then you find a narrow fissure in the tunnel wall and press yourself into it to avoid being seen. Unaware, the Eldenoran walks past your hiding place and goes to check the tunnel entrance. But the dog does not follow him. He halts close to where you are hiding and begins to sniff the dank tunnel air.

Pick a number from the Random Number Table. If you possess the Grand Master Discipline of Assimilance, add 5 to the number you have picked. If you possess Grand Huntmastery, add 2.

If your total score is now 7 or less, turn to 245.

If it is 8 or higher, turn to 278.
