In an attempt to distract the Eldenoran archers, you call upon your advanced Kai Discipline to create the sound of thunder which you project towards the end of the bridge, on the Salonese side of the river.14

Pick a number from the Random Number Table. You may add 1 to this number for every level of Kai rank you have attained above the rank of Kai Grand Master Senior.

If your total score is now 6 or lower, turn to 275.

If it is 7 or higher, turn to 306.

[14] Due to game design issues, it is impossible to reach this section of the book (cf. Section 174). It is an oversight which is not easily correctable.
