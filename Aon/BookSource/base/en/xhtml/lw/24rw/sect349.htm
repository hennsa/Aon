You inspect the vials closely and your advanced Kai skills help you to determine that the strange jelly-like substances contained within these glass tubes possess strong psychic properties.

You cannot accurately gauge the strength of these psychic potions, but you are able to determine their effects. The contents of the Red Vial, when swallowed before psychic combat, will increase your mental defences. The contents of the Blue Vial will boost your psychic attacks. The contents of the Yellow Vial, however, will reduce the effectiveness of your psychic defences and could render you especially vulnerable to mental assault.

If you wish to keep the Red Vial, the Blue Vial, or the Yellow Vial, record them on your Action Chart as Special Items which you carry in your tunic pocket.

To continue, turn to 17.
