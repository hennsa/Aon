‘Quit yappin’, y’mangy mutt!’ bellows a greasy-haired looter, but to no avail. You fear the feisty mongrel has detected your presence and quickly you draw upon your innate Magnakai skill of Animal Control to command him to be silent. The dog ceases to bark loudly and begins instead to whine with frustration.

‘He’s caught wind o’ something,’ growls a second looter. ‘I’m goin’ to take a look-see.’

You hear the man rise to his feet and then unsheathe a sword from its scabbard.
