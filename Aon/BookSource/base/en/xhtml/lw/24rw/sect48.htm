You raise your hand and point it at the lever. Then you recite the words of the Old Kingdom Spell Invisible Fist and you feel a surge of power run the length of your right arm. A concussive burst of energy leaps from your hand to slam against the lever, forcing it down. With a resounding crack, it breaks through the grime which has accumulated over the centuries and clogged its locking mechanism. Clouds of grit and dust billow out from its slot and, when you raise and pull the lever a second time, you discover that your use of Old Kingdom magic has served its purpose well.
