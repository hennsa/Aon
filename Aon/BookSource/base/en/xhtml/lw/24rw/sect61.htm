You draw upon your Magnakai Discipline of Psi-screen to protect your mind from this unexpected attack, but before your mental defences knit together properly, you suffer damage to the core of your mind (lose 4 ENDURANCE points).

Due to the mental damage caused by this powerful psychic attack, you must forfeit the use of one of your Grand Master Disciplines for the rest of this mission. If you survive this mission, you may restore your forfeited Discipline and use it again in a future Lone Wolf New Order adventure.

The blinding pain fades to a dull ache, but your Kai senses continue to scream out a warning. This evil entity poses a continuing threat to your body and your mind. You can barely see the creature yet its close proximity is filling you with overwhelming fear and loathing. Then you catch a second glimpse of its shadowy outline and your nerve breaks. Hurriedly you turn and run into the trees in a desperate attempt to escape from this formless creature of evil.
